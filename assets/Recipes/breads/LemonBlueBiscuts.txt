LEMON BLUEBERRY BISCUITS

2 cups all-purpose flour
1/3 cup sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder 1/2 teaspoon
baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 carton (8 ounces) lemon yogurt
1 egg, lightly beaten rine
1/4 cup butter or margarine melted
1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

GLAZE:

1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel

In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients.
Combine yogurt, egg, butter and lemon peel; stir
into dry ingredients just until moistened . Fold in
blueberries. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto a
greased baking sheet. Bake at 400' for 15-18
minutes or until lightly browned. Combine glaze
ingredients; drizzle over warm biscuits. Yield: 1
dozen.
