Oven-Baked Asparagus with Mustard Sauce

Center 2 pounds fresh asparagus on a sheet of heavy
duty foil. Dot with butter. Fold into a foil packet. Bake 30
minutes at 325*F. 

Sauce: 
Combine 1 cup Light sour cream 
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 
3 tablespoons Dijon mustard, 
2 teaspoons sugar, 
1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper. 
Microwave on HIGH 1-112 to 2 minutes. Serve over asparagus.
