2 pounds green beans, trimmed

1. In 12-inch skillet, heat 1 inch water and 1/2
teaspoon salt to boiling over high heat. Add
green beans; heat to boiling. Reduce heat to
low; simmer, uncovered, 5 to 10 minutes,
until beans are tendercrisp. Drain and rinse
beans with cold running water to stop
cooking; drain well. Wipe skillet dry.

2. In same skillet, melt margarine or butter
over mediUm-high heat. Add mushrooms and
shallots; cook until browned, about 15
minutes, stirring occasionally. Add flour,
pepper, and 112 teaspoon salt, and cook I
minute longer, stirring. Reduce heat to low;
stir in green beans and half-and-half and heat
through. Makes 10 accompaniment servings.
