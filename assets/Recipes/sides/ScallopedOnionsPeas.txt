Scalloped Onions and Peas

1/4 cup butter or margarine

3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard 
1/2 teaspoon salt 
1-1/2 cups milk
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
3 jars (15 ounces each) white onions, drained
1 package (10 ounces) frozen 	peas
1/2 cup sliced blanched almonds
3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese Paprika

In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Stir in flour, mustard and 
salt; cook and
stir until bubbly. Stir in milk and Worcestershire sauce; cook and stir 
until thickened, about
2 minutes. Gently stir in onions, peas and almonds. Pour into an ungreased 
2-qt. baking
dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan and paprika. Cover and bake at 3500 for 30 
minutes. Yield:
8-10 servings.
