Cranberry  WildRice Pilaf

3/4 cup uncooked wild rice
3 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup pearl barley
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup dried currants
1 tablespoon butter or margarine 
1/3 cup sliced almonds, toasted

Rinse and drain rice; place in a saucepan. Add broth and bring to a boil. 
Reduce heat; cover and
simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from the heat; stir in barley, cranberries, 
currants and butter. Spoon
into a greased 1-1/2-qt. baking dish. Cover and bake at 325' for 55 minutes 
or until liquid is absorbed
and rice is tender. Add almonds and fluff with a fork. Yield: 6-8 servings.
