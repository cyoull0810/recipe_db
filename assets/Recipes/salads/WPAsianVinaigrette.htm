Serves 1/2 cup



3 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons peanut oil
1 tablespoon toasted Asian sesame oil
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper


This simple dressing gives a light yet rich Asian flavor to salad greens. You'll find rice wine vinegar and toasted sesame oil in the Asian foods section of well-stocked markets.




1. In a small bowl, whisk together the vinegar, soy sauce, peanut oil, sesame oil, and lemon juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper. reserve until ready to use.
