Festive Cranberry Salad

1 can (114 ounces) sweetened condensed milk 
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 can (20 ounces) crushed pineapple, drained
1 can (116 ounces) whole-berry cranberry sauce
2 cups miniature marshmallows 
1/2 cup chopped pecans
Red food coloring, optional
I carton (8 ounces) frozen whipped topping, thawed

In a bowl, combine milk and lemon juice; mix well. Stir in the pineapple,
cranberry sauce, marshmallows, pecans and food coloring if desired. Fold
in whipped topping. Spoon into a 13-in. x 9-in x 2in. baking dish. Freeze 
until
firm, 4 hours or overnight. Cut into squares. Yield: 12-16 servings.
