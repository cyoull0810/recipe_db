The KETEL ONE Drink List

martini

5 PARTS KETEL ONE VODKA

1PART DRY VERMOUTH

Stir in a mixing glass until well-chilled and strain into a martini glass.

Garnish with an olive or a lemon peel.

autumn wood

5 PARTS KETEL ONE VODKA

1 PART YELLOW CHARTREUSE

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass.

Garnish with a cocktail cherry.

elegance

3 PARTS KETEL ONE VODKA

3 PARTS RED PORT

Stir with ice and strain into a cocktail glass.

Garnish with an orange twist.

nutty martini

5 PARTS KETEL ONE VODKA

1 PART FRANGELICO

Stir with ice and pour into a chilled martini glass.

sea breeze

6 PARTS KETEL ONE VODKA

CRANBERRY JUICE

GRAPEFRUIT JUICE

Pour KETEL ONE VODKA over ice in a highball glass and fill with equal

parts of cranberry juice and grapefruit juice. 

Garnish with an orange wedge and a cherry.

KETEL ONE me

2 PARTS KETEL ONE

1 PART CRÉME DE BANANAS

1 PART FRESH CREAM

DASH GRENADINE ORANGE JUICE

Shake all the ingredients with ice and strain into a highball glass.

Garnish with an orange spiral and a red cherry.

mandrico

1 PART KETEL ONE

COCA-COLA

Build over ice in a highball glass.

mandolito

3 PARTS KETEL ONE

2 PARTS LEMON JUICE

1 PART SUGAR SYRUP

DASH BLUE CURACAO

Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Top with a dash of Blue Curacao

passion

2 PARTS KETEL ONE

1 PART PASSOA PINNEAPPLE JUICE

Build over ice in a highball glass.

Garnish with a pineapple wedge.

blossom

3 PARTS KETEL ONE

1 PART STRAWBERRY SYRUP

2 PARTS ORANGE JUICE

 SODA WATER

Shake and strain into an ice-filled highball glass.

Fill up with soda water. Garnish with a strawberry.

1 PART KETEL ONE

DASH GRENADINE

1 TEASPOON GRANULATED SUGAR

Crush finely minced orange and granulated sugar with a muddler in an Old Fashioned glass.

Add ingredients and fill up with crushed ice. Stir gently and serve.

margarita

2 PARTS KETEL ONE CITRON

1 PART COINTREAU

 2 DASHES FRESH LIME JUICE

Shake with ice and strain into a well-chilled cocktail

glass with a salted rim. (Tip: coat rim of glass with

lime cordial and roll on plate of salt).

madam

2 PARTS KETEL ONE CITRON

1 PART MELON LIQUEUR

1 PART LIME/LEMON LIQUEUR EXOTIC NECTAR JUICE

Shake all ingredients with crushed ice and serve in

a highball glass. Garnish with lime spiral,

an orange wedge and mint leaves.

madrid

1 PART KETEL ONE CITRON

1 PART MELON LIQUEUR

1 PART GRAPEFRUIT JUICE_

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled

cocktail glass.

 

daiquiri

1PART KETEL ONE 2 DASHES LIME JUICE

DASH SUGAR SYRUP FRESH STRAWBERRIES Mix with ice in

a blender and strain

into a chilled cocktail glass.

Garnish with a strawberry.

Press a slice of lime over the drink.

lemon drop

4 PARTS KETEL ONE CITRON LEMON

Pour KETEL ONE CITRON into a shot glass.

Dip a wedge of lemon into sugar.

Drink and bite.

devil

3 PARTS KETEL ONE

3 PARTS YELLOW CHARTREUSE

Stir with ice and  strain into a chilled martini glass.

Garnish with jalapeño pepper.

Bloody

6 PARTS KETEL ONE

12 PARTS TOMATO JUICE

2 DASHES OF WORCHESTERSHIRE SAUCE

4 DASHES OF TABASCO

DASH OF LEMON JUICE

A PINCH OF SALT AND PEPPER

Roll all ingredients back and forth in between two sturdy glasses.

Pour over ice into a large goblet.

Garnish with a lemon and a lime wedge and/or a celery stalk.

bloody ceasar

6 PARTS KETEL ONE

12 PARTS CLAMATO JUICE

 2 DASHES OF WORCHESTERSHIRE SAUCE

 4 DASHES OF TABASCO

DASH OF LEMON JUICE

A PINCH OF SALT AND PEPPER

Roll all ingredients back and forth in between

two sturdy glasses.

Pour over ice into a large goblet.

Garnish with a lemon and a lime wedge and/or a celery stalk.

hot & frozen

KETEL ONE

5 FRESH STRAWBERRIES

A BUNCH OF FRESH BASIL

Mix with ice in a blender until smooth.

Serve in a cocktail glass.

cosmopolitan

4 PARTS KETEL ONE CITRON

2 PARTS COINTREAU

1 PART CRANBERRY JUICE

DASH LIME JUICE

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
