APPLE CRISP A LA FANNIE FARMER

4 cups peeled, sliced tart apples

1/2 cup apple cider

3/4 cup flour

1 cup white or brown sugar

1 teaspoon cinnamon

1/2 cup butter

1/4 teaspoon salt

Whipped cream optional as garnish

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Butter a deep baking dish. Mix apples with cider. Pour apples and cider into pan.

Mix remaining ingredients with a fork. Spread over apples, bake at 350 degrees until apples are tender and crust is brown, about 30 minutes.
