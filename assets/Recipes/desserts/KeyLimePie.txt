Key Lime Pie
Yield:  (1) 8- or 9-inch pie

1 8- or 9-inch baked pastry shell or graham cracker crust
3 egg yolks
1 (14 ounce) can Sweetened Condensed Milk
½ cup Lime Juice

Separate eggs and place yolks in bowl.  Stir in can of condensed milk and lime juice.

Pour into crust and bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.

Whip egg whites with 1/8 teaspoon Cream of Tartar and ¼ cup sugar

Put meringue on top of cooked pie and continue to bake until lightly browned on top.

Chill completely before serving.
